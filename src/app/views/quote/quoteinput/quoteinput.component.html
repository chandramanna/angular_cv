Different Types Of Car Insurance Plans

In general, you will be offered two types motor insurance covers by a car insurance company within their car insurance plans. The Two types of motor insurance plans are Third Party Liability Car Insurance Coverage and Comprehensive Car Insurance Coverage.


Third Party Liability Car Insurance

The first party who is the owner of a four wheeler buys a motor insurance policy from the second party that is the car insurance company for the protection against the claims of a third party that can be a driver of another vehicle, passengers or even pedestrians. Third Party car insurance policy is specifically designed to cover any property damages or bodily injuries to a third party caused by your vehicle. It’s obligatory to have third party car insurance for all motor vehicles piling on road in India.

Comprehensive Car Insurance

Comprehensive car insurance is an optional car insurance policy that comes with higher level of protection for your vehicle. The best advantage of buying comprehensive car insurance in India is that it protects your own vehicle against various damages arising from accident, fire, vandalism or even theft plus it also takes care of the third party liability claims.

Add On Covers For Four Wheeler Insurance

Like the name suggests, add on covers for motor insurance stand for additional coverage that are not included with a comprehensive auto insurance plan. To purchase add on cover for your vehicle, you’ll have to make some extra payment along with your car insurance premium. Best car insurance companies in India offer auto insurance policy buyers with a broad range of effective add on covers and attractive discounts. Car insurance company offers various add on covers from which you can pick as per your requirements for your four wheeler insurance. You can choose one or multiple add on covers for your auto insurance. Including add on covers in your motor insurance policy is subject to your car insurance premium. Some of the best add on covers for car insurance policies are following.

Zero Depreciation

The car insurance company will calculate depreciation on certain parts of your vehicle at the time of claim settlement. This procedure leads to getting less claim amount. However, purchasing Zero Depreciation or Nil Depreciation cover for your auto insurance policy entitles you to get the full amount on your claim.

Roadside Assistance

No Claim Bonus (NCB)

No Claim Bonus is an additional discount on premium offered by car insurance companies to their policyholders for not filing any claim during a policy term. Car Insurance Claim Process

The reason you compare car insurance online and buy insurance online is to get financial assistance from your car insurance company when you make cashless claims on your online motor insurance policy. Auto insurance companies pay out financial compensation when they approve cashless claims. So, you have to file a valid cashless auto insurance claim to get it approved by your insurer. Since, cashless auto insurance claim process differs in different situations, you have to be aware of all the claim procedures so that you can make valid cashless insurance claim and get assistance from your car insurance company in the time of need.

Car Insurance Online Cashless Claim Process for Damage to the Insured Vehicle:

In case of massive damage, the following are the steps to make a successful cashless car insurance claim with your motor insurance provider:
  • Intimate your insurer at the earliest and register your auto insurance claim.
  • Lodge an FIR in police station mentioning the whole incident in details to strengthen your cashless claim.
  • You have to keep your vehicle on the accident spot until your insurer sends a surveyor for the inspection.
  • Your cashless auto insurance claim process will start, once the surveyor submit a report to the car insurance company.
  • If the damage is minor, here are the steps to file a cashless motor insurance claim
  • You can take away your car and intimate to car insurance company about the accidents
  • Assess the extent of your loss and file claim with your car insurance company accordingly
  • Your insurance provider will send surveyor to assess the loss.
  • Cashless motor insurance claim process will start after the authorization by the surveyor.
For cashless motor insurance claims, you can get your damaged insured vehicle from the nearest cashless garage in the network of your car insurer. You can also make motor insurance reimbursement claim on your online car insurance policy in India.
